Tracking Data of Covid-19 at U.S. Colleges and Universities


The data in this document contains counts of Covid-19 cases reported on college and university campuses in the United States during the 2020-21 academic year.

Source: The New York Times 

Link: https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2021/us/college-covid-tracker.html

This data was most recently updated on May 26, 2021.
